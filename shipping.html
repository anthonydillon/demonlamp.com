Shipping costs

  • UK & Northern Ireland £8
  • Europe £20

Exporting Outside the UK

Demon lamp will work in any country with a 230 - 240 volt supply, as long as you use a UK 3 Pin plug to 2 Pin Plug Adapter. Look at the map above, if your country is green it will work.

On orders exporting to countries outside the EU which use 110 Volts, for example USA, Canada and Mexico, the energy saving bulb holder will be replaced with a standard E12 cord set with 2 pin plug. Unfortunately we cannot supply the bulb with this.

If you order goods for delivery outside the UK, they may be subject to import duties and taxes which are levied when the delivery reaches the specified destination. You will be responsible for payment of any such import duties and taxes. Please note that we have no control over these charges and cannot predict their amount. Please also note that you must comply with all applicable laws and regulations of the Country for which the goods are destined. We will not be liable for any breach by you of any such laws.

Return/Cancellation policy

If for any reason you are unhappy with your purchase please inform us, by email, within 24 hours of receiving the goods. Any damages must be reported by email within 24 hours of the goods being received.

Goods returned due to a fault will be refunded in full, including a refund of the delivery charges for sending the item to you and the cost incurred by you in returning the item to us.

Delivery policy

Please note the goods will not be shipped until we have received full payment, including delivery charges.

Please allow upto 30 days for delivery on International orders and upto 7 working days on UK orders. Your order will be dispatched for delivery by courier.

All Demon Lamp orders to the UK, however many products you buy, will have a delivery cost of £8. The same rules apply to orders for delivery within the EU, delivery charged at £20, and orders for International delivery are charged at £35.

Please note: The price charged for delivery of any item includes the cost of postage, packaging materials and order processing costs. The postage price seen on the package is only a part of these costs.
