Here are answers to some common questions about the Demon Lamp product. These FAQs cover a small range of questions but if you have a question not answered below please contact us at info@demonlamp.com.

Is it possible to purchase a Demon Lamp via PayPal?
A PayPal payment system will be added to the website in the coming weeks.
Is it possible to get delivery concessions if ordering 2 or more lamps?
Sorry no we can not give a discount. We get no discount on any less than 12 lamps being dispatched. It is considered to be a large package particularly for international delivery. We are not a large company and we are not dispatching a large number of units. So we have no special deals in place with couriers.
Is the VAT included in the price?
Yes
Is the Shipping cost included in the price?
No, see shipping costs on the website www.demonlamps.com/shipping and/or in FAQ question below.
What are the shipping costs to my country?
Shipping Cost:
UK/Northern Ireland £8
Europe £20
International £45
What is it made of?
Polypropolene (plastic)
We would have preferred to produce it in glass, but unfortunately it cost too much. We thought very carefully before releasing a plastic product out in the world.
Does it take batteries?
No
Can we keep it outside?
Please do not use the lamp outside.
The light bulb supplied with the unit was damaged on delivery. Can you replace it?
Yes of course just drop us an email with your order number, and we will send out a new one.
Whats the minimum quantity on wholesale orders?
12 Lamps
Whats the unit cost on wholesale orders?
It varies depending on order size, so please contact us for more details at info@demonlamp.com
